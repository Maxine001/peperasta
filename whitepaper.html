WHITE PAPER

Establishing connections within the cryptocurrency space can be quite a difficult task.
While current social media platforms do offer certain networking opportunities, most are
inefficient at best, and ineffective at worst. A decentralized social media networking
platform would offer an outlet for cryptocurrency developers, marketers, investors, and
projects to showcase achievements and skills, build connections, and create trust, all in
a single place. The following whitepaper will provide an overview of the core fundamentals
of the DecentraLink platform and ecosystem, detailing the many ways in which it advantages users.
